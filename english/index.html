HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Download MetaMask or the wallet of your choice from the app store or Google Play Store for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension at metamask.io.

Get Some BNB

Have BNB in your wallet to exchange for $KHABY. If you don't have BNB, you can buy it directly on MetaMask, transfer it from another wallet, or buy it on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to PancakeSwap

Connect to PancakeSwap. Go to pancakeswap.finance in Google Chrome or in the browser within your MetaMask application. Connect your wallet. Paste the $KHABY token address into PancakeSwap, select KHABY, and confirm. When MetaMask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
